## The two DWPA root causes (both fixed)

1. **EP baseline read an empty season.** `league_ep()`'s no-arg default filters
   `season='Current'`, which post-rollover holds nothing → the possession model
   scored every possession against **EP = 0.0**: misses and turnovers earned the
   defense nothing while makes charged it in full. `season_wpa` now passes its
   own gender+season game set (also keeps boys/girls baselines separate).
2. **Orphaned positive credit.** Unforced turnovers (no stealer) and forced
   misses with no credited defensive rebound were dropped entirely, while every
   made basket charged `guarded_by`. Possession mode now team-splits: orphaned
   stops split among the on-floor defenders (lineup snapshots cover 100% of
   events), and a made basket charges the on-ball defender 60% with the help
   absorbing the rest (`ONBALL_SHARE`, sweepable).

**Result:** girls 4+GP pool went from *everyone negative* to **25 positive / 15
negative** (sum ≈ 0 by construction), with the known defensive anchors on top
(Schwerdfeger +1.32, Bond +0.99). Boys 2+GP: 6 of 14 positive.

## Game-timing context (late fouling helps no one)

`helpers/late_game.py` — one shared detector: a foul by the trailing team, down
1–10, inside the final 2:00 of Q4/OT = strategic clock-stop. Wired so:

- the shooter's WPA from those free throws pays 0.25× (`STRATEGIC_FT_DAMP`),
- the fouler's discipline read uses `nsPF` (strategic fouls excluded),
- official profiles and crew tendencies exclude the calls (surfaced as
  `strategic_calls`),
- garbage time needed no mechanism — verified by test that a late blowout
  basket is worth ~0 WPA.

### Adopted (aggressive, evidence-backed)

- `team_ratings.DEFAULT_REG` **2.0 → 0.5** — walk-forward MAE falls
  monotonically with less shrink; 0.5 keeps a divergence guard for the sparse
  November graph the harness can't test (0.15 scored better still; revisit).
- `team_ratings.DEFAULT_SOS_WEIGHT` **0.8 → 1.6** — monotone gain to 1.6, flat
  beyond.
- `player_ratings.RATING_K_GAMES` **3 → 2** — T4 LOGO favors less shrink on the
  deeper book; 1-game cameos still retain only ~26% of their edge.
- `player_ratings.TEAM_PRIOR_LAMBDA` **0.35 → 0.5**.
- OVERALL **penalty leaves** `TOV/Gz` + `nsPF/Gz` at weight **0.2** — the
  "negative weights": 0.2 beat 0.4 and 0.7 on held-out Game Score.
- **Archetype anchors** (`ARCH_ANCHOR_BLEND = 0.5`): thin players shrink toward
  a 50/50 blend of team prior and their k-means archetype mean (style-only
  features, no circularity). Validated: turning it off scored worst.

### Rejected by the gates (built, measured, not adopted)

- **Hybrid tracked-team ramp** (`team_ratings.hybrid_ratings`): blending tracked
  possession ratings into score ratings *worsened* walk-forward MAE at every K
  (F 10.571 → 10.60–10.70). The tracked book (~39 games) doesn't yet beat the
  6,000-game score book for margins. Ships opt-in; re-gate as tracking grows.
- Heavier `impact` (0.9→1.2) and `offense` (1.1→1.3) pillars — both hurt T2.
- Penalty weight 0.7 — swamps the production signal.

### Not swept (no honest gate)

`ONBALL_SHARE`, `STRATEGIC_FT_DAMP`, `WINDOW_SECS` are credit-assignment design
constants — no harness target measures credit quality. All registered in the

## Officials — win-probability call context (objective only)

`officials.wp_profile`, merged into `official_overview` (68 refs profiled):

- `avg_call_li` — normalized leverage of the moments a ref blows the whistle,
- `hi_li_calls` / `li_call_ratio` — their high-leverage call share vs the
  high-leverage moment share of their own games (>1 = keeps the whistle in big
  moments; <1 = swallows it) — the "calling the clutch foul takes guts" number
  with zero subjectivity,
- `foulouts` + `foulout_impact` — fouled-out player's in-game scoring share ×
  game remaining ("fouled out the best player with 5:00 left" context),
- strategic clock-stop calls excluded everywhere, counted as `strategic_calls`.
